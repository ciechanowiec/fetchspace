Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 1 December 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how "Fetch Space" (the "Service") collects, uses, and protects your data. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy and applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR"). If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, you should discontinue use of the Service immediately.

1. Data We Collect

Through Telegram’s interface, we may collect and process the following information:

  • Uploaded Files: When you upload files via our Telegram bot, we temporarily store those files on our servers to generate a public sharing link.
  • Metadata: This can include timestamps of uploads, file types, file sizes, and links generated for sharing.
  • Telegram User Information: We do not collect identifying personal information beyond what Telegram shares with bots by default (e.g., user ID, username if provided). No direct personal data such as email or phone number is requested or retained by us.

2. Purpose and Legal Basis of Processing

We process personal data only as necessary to provide and improve our Services. The legal bases for our processing under the GDPR include:

  • Performance of a Contract: Data is processed to deliver the service of file uploading and link generation.
  • Legitimate Interests: We may process metadata to maintain system security, prevent abuse, and improve service functionality.
  • Consent: By using the Service, you consent to the processing of uploaded files and related metadata as described in this Policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

We primarily use your data to provide you with file-sharing functionality. Specifically, we use the data to:

  • Store and host the files you upload so that public links can be generated.
  • Respond to your commands, such as /clear, to delete your stored files.
  • Maintain and enhance the security and reliability of our systems.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We only share or disclose data if:

  • Legal Compliance: We are required to comply with a legal obligation or respond to lawful requests by public authorities, including meeting national security or law enforcement requirements.
  • Service Providers: We rely on reputable hosting providers. They may have access to data only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in any country where we have facilities or service providers. If we transfer personal data to countries outside the European Economic Area ("EEA"), we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or an equivalent legal mechanism to protect your rights and freedoms.

6. Data Retention

We only retain uploaded files and associated metadata for as long as necessary to provide the Service. When you issue the /clear command, your files and associated data are deleted. We may retain system logs and related data for a limited period for security and auditing purposes.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you reside in or are located in the EEA, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Erasure: You can request the deletion of your data when it’s no longer necessary for the purposes collected.
  • Restriction: You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Objection: You can object to our processing of your personal data if our basis for processing is legitimate interest.
  • Portability: You can request a structured, machine-readable copy of your personal data for transfer to another service.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address provided below. We will respond to your request within the time frames required by applicable data protection laws.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us to remove that data.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and where appropriate, we will notify you via a Telegram message or other channels. Your continued use of the Service after such changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact us through the official channels provided in our bot description or via email at herman@ciechanowiec.eu.

By using Fetch Space, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy, understand it, and agree to be bound by its terms. We are committed to processing personal data responsibly and in compliance with GDPR and other applicable privacy regulations.
